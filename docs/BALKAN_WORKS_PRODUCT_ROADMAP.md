# Balkan.works Product Roadmap

## Purpose

Define product evolution from the first local MVP to a governed AI platform. The principle is to build value step by step: not the largest product, but the most useful one at the current stage of the network.

```text
Directory → Discovery Platform → Business Growth Platform → Marketplace → AI Economy Platform
```

## Phase 0 — Foundation (pre-MVP)

Create the brand and domain foundation, design system, technical architecture, database, core data models and secure development process. The output is a stable base on which MVP learning can happen quickly.

## Phase 1 — MVP: local discovery (months 0–3)

**Goal:** a person can find a relevant local business and a business can receive a meaningful contact.

- User accounts: registration, login and profile.
- Business profiles: name, description, category, location, contact and images.
- Search and filters by category and location.
- Categories for services, products and places.
- Basic review capability only when moderation and trust controls are ready.
- Essential events for search, profile view, contact and return use.

**Success evidence:** users find businesses, businesses receive contacts, and both sides demonstrate repeat activity.

## Phase 2 — Trust and engagement (months 3–6)

**Goal:** increase confidence and give users a reason to return.

- Proportionate business verification and trust signals.
- Favorites for businesses and offers.
- Notifications for relevant changes, recommendations and offers, subject to consent settings.
- Business dashboard with profile views, contacts and basic statistics.

## Phase 3 — Business growth platform (months 6–12)

**Goal:** demonstrate a paid business-growth product only after free value is visible.

- Premium profiles and additional business content.
- Promotions, sponsored placement and measurable campaigns.
- Analytics explaining interest, searches and outcomes without exposing private user data.
- Lead-management workflows for customer enquiries and contacts.

## Phase 4 — Marketplace (months 12–24)

**Goal:** move from discovery to completing a service need where density, trust and legal readiness justify it.

- Booking and availability flows.
- Permissioned business/customer messaging.
- Payments through qualified providers and governed transaction records.
- End-to-end flow: **search → select → book → pay → review**.

Marketplace work remains gated by sufficient local supply, active user demand, trust controls, consumer protection and payment/legal review.

## Phase 5 — AI platform (months 24+)

**Goal:** make the platform an intelligent assistant grounded in permissioned local data.

- AI search for natural-language needs, such as a family restaurant with parking.
- Explainable recommendations with relevant alternatives.
- Business assistant for content drafting, marketing suggestions and governed analysis.
- Future agents that can carry out constrained, approved tasks such as comparing options or initiating a booking.

AI capability is introduced only with quality event data, privacy controls, evaluation, human escalation and clear user disclosure.

## Intentional delays

Delay complex marketplace work until network density exists; advanced AI until data quality exists; uncontrolled category expansion until a focused local model works; and international rollout until the city model is proven.

## Product priority gate

Before committing to a feature, answer:

1. Does it increase meaningful user value or activation?
2. Does it improve measurable business value?
3. Does it increase trust or local data quality?
4. Does it strengthen a defensible advantage?

If the evidence is weak across these questions, do not build it yet.

## Metrics by outcome

- **Discovery:** searches, relevance and time to a useful result.
- **Engagement:** profile/offer views, favorites and return frequency.
- **Connection:** calls, messages, enquiries and other successful connections.
- **Business value:** leads, qualified conversions, retention and ROI evidence.

## Final vision

Balkan.works evolves from a place where people search for businesses into a platform that understands local needs and connects people to the most useful trusted solution.
