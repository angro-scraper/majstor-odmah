# Balkan.works Master Roadmap

## Principle

Build foundational local value first, then add layers only when evidence supports them. A roadmap is a prioritization tool, not a promise to release every module on a fixed date.

## Phase 0 — Foundation (month 0–1)

Complete the decision framework: vision, architecture, design language, database/data plan, repository/runtime baseline, secure environments and launch-city research. Prepare a business target list, partner approach and operational onboarding playbook.

## Phase 1 — MVP Core (months 1–4)

Deliver users, profiles, location, search, businesses, offers, business dashboard and admin moderation. Establish reliable APIs, migrations, tests, analytics and support processes. Do not ship Wallet, Marketplace or complex autonomous AI before the Core loop works.

## Phase 2 — Pilot City (months 4–6)

Target a working local network: roughly 100 active businesses, 1,000 users and 500 fresh offers. Freeze broad scope; improve UX, speed, onboarding, support, content quality and search success. The decision to proceed depends on retention and business outcomes, not registrations alone.

## Phase 3 — First Revenue (months 6–12)

Introduce transparent Business Premium and paid Digital Flyers/featured promotions only after their value is measurable. Add basic business analytics and carefully evaluated recommendations. Track revenue, unit economics, churn and consumer trust.

## Phase 4 — City Expansion (year 2)

Replicate a proven city playbook with local operations, ambassadors and city-specific supply. Add Save Food as the first major vertical only where food partners, fulfilment and consumer support are ready.

## Phase 5 — Business Platform (years 2–3)

Activate Opsnestone capabilities such as CRM, invoices, inventory and analytics behind correct tenant/security controls. Introduce business AI for drafts, insights and recommendations with human approval.

## Phase 6 — Marketplace Ecosystem (years 3–5)

Add Marketplace and later Wallet/rewards/payments only after trust, legal, payment-provider, dispute and support capabilities are proven.

## Phase 7 — AI Platform (years 4–6)

Evolve grounded search into user, business and analytics agents that operate through the AI Gateway, governed data and explicit approvals.

## Phase 8 — Regional Scale (years 5–10)

Expand country by country with localization, compliant operations, local teams and strategic partners. Develop enterprise capabilities from proven integration needs.

## Priority matrix

- **Must have now:** users, businesses, offers, search, moderation and reliability.
- **Should have next:** recommendations, rewards and Save Food after the Core loop is proven.
- **Later:** Wallet, Marketplace, Travel and other verticals after their prerequisites are met.

## Gate for every module

Every proposal must state the problem, user, network effect, revenue model, data/security impact, operating cost, success metric and rollback path. Reject features that do not create a clear outcome or undermine focus.

## Milestones

1. Reviewable MVP Core.
2. One city with a working supply/demand loop.
3. First paying businesses with retained value.
4. Repeatable multi-city operations.
5. A compliant, trusted regional network.
