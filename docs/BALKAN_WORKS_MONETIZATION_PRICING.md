# Balkan.works Monetization & Pricing Strategy

## Purpose

Define how Balkan.works monetizes incremental value for businesses and, later, users and partners through subscriptions, visibility, campaigns, transactions and governed AI tools.

## Principle

Create and demonstrate value before charging for additional value. Basic discovery remains useful for consumers; businesses can enter the network without a payment barrier. Pricing must be transparent, locally appropriate, testable and supported by real entitlements — never by unreleased promises.

`Subscriptions + premium visibility + transparent campaigns + future transactions + governed AI → revenue ecosystem`

## Customer side

### Free consumer experience

Core local discovery, business/offer browsing, basic recommendations and favorites support network participation. These features are not turned into a confusing paywall.

### Future consumer premium

Potential incremental value includes advanced assistance, richer personalization, member benefits or rewards. It is considered only after preference controls, AI quality, reward economics, consumer terms and clear cancellation/support experience are ready.

## Business package architecture

| Package | Intended customer | Value | Availability condition |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free Business | Local business entering the network | Basic verified-eligible profile, category/location and constrained offer capability | MVP activation baseline |
| Business Plus | Active local business seeking additional discoverability | More current offers, eligible visibility options, basic performance insight | Test only after businesses see measurable local value |
| Business Pro | Growth-oriented business | Advanced analytics, campaign workflow and future approved AI/business tools | Requires mature entitlement, analytics and support systems |
| Enterprise | Chains, strategic partners and complex organizations | Multi-location controls, support, integration and contracted reporting | Requires security, legal, integration and account-management readiness |

Exact prices, usage limits, taxes, currencies, trials and cancellation terms are not fixed by this document. They are country-aware experiments that require cost analysis, customer research, legal/accounting review and a documented pricing decision before public release.

## Premium visibility and campaigns

Businesses may later buy clearly labeled local promotion: featured business placement, promoted offers or defined local campaigns. Ranking separates organic relevance from paid placement. Campaign delivery includes eligibility, budget/targeting controls, frequency limits, transparent labels, measurement and anti-fraud/moderation rules. User location/interests are used only within approved privacy and consent settings.

## Future transactional revenue

| Module | Potential value exchange | Preconditions |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Marketplace | Commission or seller-tool fee on completed transactions | Payment provider, refunds/disputes, seller policy, reconciliation and consumer protection |
| Save Food | Commission, partner plan or premium listing | Reservation/pickup flow, merchant rules, support and transaction controls |
| Wallet/loyalty | Eligible payment/partner/reward economics | Regulated-provider/legal review, ledger, fraud, privacy and accounting controls |
| AI services | Consumer/business assistance and analytics | Data governance, model quality, cost controls, disclosure and human approval |
| Insights | Aggregated market intelligence | Privacy-preserving aggregation, contracts and enterprise governance |

No payment, wallet or data-product model is activated merely because the architecture allows it.

## Pricing design principles

- **Accessible:** viable for small local businesses in the target city/country.
- **Value-linked:** each paid entitlement answers a concrete business need.
- **Scalable:** larger organizations pay for genuinely greater scope, usage or support.
- **Transparent:** price, tax treatment, renewal, limits, cancellation and support are clear before purchase.
- **Fair:** paid visibility is labeled and does not erase useful organic discovery.
- **Sustainable:** price covers direct delivery, support, infrastructure, risk and required compliance costs.

## Activation sequence

1. Establish accurate free profiles, local supply and customer discovery.
2. Validate willingness to pay for optional visibility or promotion with small controlled pilots.
3. Add subscription tools where ongoing business results and support capacity exist.
4. Introduce transactions only after payment/legal/operations readiness.
5. Add premium AI/insight products after quality, privacy and unit-economics proof.

Each step is feature-flagged, measured and reversible.

## Pricing research and experiments

Before public pricing, test customer problem, package comprehension, perceived value, service cost, local purchasing power, conversion, retention, support load and churn. An experiment records audience/city, package, price hypothesis, time window, primary metric, guardrails and decision. Avoid dark patterns, forced upgrades, misleading “discounts” or changing a subscribed customer's terms without clear notice.

## Revenue flywheel and metrics

`More active businesses → more current offers → more useful consumer discovery → more governed data/relevance → stronger business value → sustainable revenue`

Track MRR, ARR, ARPU/ARPA, paid conversion, upgrade/downgrade, gross margin, churn, retention, CAC, LTV methodology, payback period, campaign performance, transaction take rate when applicable and feature-level cost-to-serve. Review results by cohort, city, business category and plan; do not optimize short-term conversion at the expense of local network health.

## Commercial safeguards

Subscriptions need a billing owner, entitlements service, invoice/tax handling, renewal/cancellation flow, support and audit trail before release. Enterprise commitments require contracts and service boundaries. Advertising/campaigns require disclosure and controls. Financial data, payment flows and rewards receive qualified legal, accounting, security and provider review.

## Final statement

Balkan.works earns by helping businesses achieve more value and helping users discover it. The strongest model is diversified, but each revenue line is activated only when it is useful, trusted, lawful and operationally sustainable.
