# Balkan.works Investment Pitch

## Purpose

Provide a concise investment narrative for qualified investor conversations. It explains the Balkan.works vision, problem, solution, business model and evidence plan. It is not a public offer, financial guarantee or substitute for legal, financial or market diligence.

## Company vision

Balkan.works is building a digital ecosystem that connects people, local businesses and future business tools in one modular platform. The long-term aim is a leading regional layer for discovering, connecting and growing local economies.

## The problem

People can struggle to find quality, current local businesses because information is scattered across many channels, trust signals are inconsistent and relevant local value is hard to compare. Small and medium businesses can struggle to reach new customers, operate costly fragmented marketing/tools and access useful insight for decisions.

## The opportunity

The opportunity is a large regional base of people with recurring local needs and local businesses seeking digital growth. Market size, digital adoption, category density and competitor claims must be supported with current, source-linked research by target country/city before external presentation.

## The solution

Balkan.works connects:

`People + businesses + trust + governed data + responsible AI`

For users, it offers local discovery, current offers, helpful recommendations and simpler decisions. For businesses, it offers a digital profile, discoverability, promotion, factual customer-interest insight and future approved business tools.

## Product ecosystem

`Core platform → offers/discovery → business tools → selected transaction modules → wallet/rewards integrations → intelligence platform`

The Core begins with Balkan ID, business profiles, locations/categories, search and offers. Marketplace, wallet, advanced accounting/ops tools and autonomous agents are later modules activated only after their dependencies, operations, legal/security controls and market evidence are ready.

## Why now

Potential timing drivers are ongoing business digitization, demand for reliable local discovery, practical AI-assisted workflows and consumers' expectation of relevant digital experiences. These are hypotheses to substantiate with dated regional evidence; the product must solve a concrete local need regardless of technology trends.

## Business model

Potential revenue is diversified:

- Business SaaS subscriptions for proven tools.
- Optional premium visibility and transparent local campaigns.
- Future marketplace/reservation transaction fees.
- Premium AI assistance and analytics under governance.
- Enterprise/partner integrations and solutions.

Monetization follows demonstrated value. Early free participation builds supply; paid services require clear pricing, entitlement, billing/support, legal and financial controls.

## Network effect

`More active businesses → more current offers/content → more successful user discovery → more business outcomes → more participation and data quality`

The asset is not raw traffic or generic AI. It is a trusted, current local network, strengthened by business workflows, partner relationships, data governance and better relevance.

## Competitive advantage

1. **Local network:** Dense, city-by-city active supply and community relevance.
2. **Business enablement:** More than a listing; businesses can see/manage local value.
3. **Trust layer:** Accurate information, verification discipline, reviews and moderation.
4. **Data/AI layer:** Permissioned data and evaluated intelligence improve discovery over time.
5. **Modular ecosystem:** Shared identity, contracts and design let future products reinforce the core.

These advantages must be proven through activation, retention, business outcomes and operational quality rather than asserted as complete today.

## Go-to-market

`One city → proof of local loop → more cities → country readiness → regional expansion`

Start with direct acquisition and activation of quality businesses, then drive user value through useful local supply, content/SEO, partnerships, community and eligible referrals. A pilot aims for hundreds of quality businesses and thousands of activated users according to city capacity; the evidence gate is retention and successful connections, not raw signup counts.

## Growth engine

Businesses create local content/offers that attract users; user activity demonstrates value to businesses; trusted partners improve distribution; quality data improves relevance. Growth is measured by activated/retained cohorts, supply freshness, local network health and economics — not paid traffic alone.

## Investment use

Capital may fund secure product development, web/mobile experience, pilot-city activation, local sales/partnerships, customer support, infrastructure, security and measured AI/data readiness. Allocation is tied to milestones, budget, owner and evidence in the Financial Model and Investor Data Room.

## Technology

The platform is designed as a modular monolith with typed web/mobile/API foundations, PostgreSQL, versioned APIs/data, feature flags, analytics, security boundaries and an AI gateway. It can scale selectively as local adoption and workload prove the need. Documentation and code foundations are not presented as deployed production capability until verified.

## Key milestones

| Stage | Evidence sought |
| --- | --- |
| MVP | Usable core local discovery/business flow with quality/security/operations baseline |
| Market fit | Activated and retained users/businesses, repeatable city learning and early paid-value evidence |
| Regional expansion | Repeatable city/country playbook, local operations and unit-economics discipline |
| Intelligence platform | Governed data, evaluated AI and practical user/business outcome lift |

## Investment thesis

Balkan.works combines local networks, business tools, trust, data and responsible AI into a platform that could become regional digital infrastructure. The investment case is the path to proving that thesis one local market at a time, with transparent risks, measurable evidence and disciplined capital use.

## Final message

We are not building another isolated application. We are building a network that can connect the region — beginning with a useful, trusted local experience.
