# Balkan.works AI Roadmap

## Purpose

Define the progressive development of Balkan.works AI capabilities. AI is not a decorative feature: it is a governed intelligence layer that should make local discovery and business outcomes more useful over time.

## Vision

> An AI assistant that understands local needs and helps businesses serve their markets better.

```text
Data collection → Smart search → Recommendations → AI assistant → AI agents → Local economy intelligence
```

## Phase 1 — Data foundation (months 0–12)

**Goal:** establish trustworthy signals before introducing AI experiences.

- versioned event tracking;
- user-intent and search-context signals;
- accurate, maintained business and service information;
- quality, privacy, retention and access controls.

Track what people seek, what they choose, whether an outcome helped and which businesses solve a need. The success measure is governed data quality, not the number of collected fields.

## Phase 2 — Smart search (months 6–18)

**Goal:** let search understand people rather than merely match keywords.

A query such as “quiet meeting place with parking nearby” should reason over language, synonyms, context, location and intent using verified platform knowledge. Start with measurable relevance improvements; preserve a conventional search fallback and do not fabricate availability or attributes.

## Phase 3 — Recommendation engine (months 12–24)

**Goal:** suggest useful options based on consented, relevant signals.

Rank options using history, location, declared preferences and behavior, while supporting diversity, recency and explainability. Measure recommendation quality through engagement, successful connections, satisfaction and long-term trust — not clicks alone.

## Phase 4 — User AI assistant (months 18–36)

**Goal:** enable a guided conversation with the local network.

For a request such as finding a good car service this week, the assistant understands the need, retrieves relevant businesses, compares supported information and explains why options are suggested. Responses cite platform facts where feasible, distinguish uncertainty and route high-stakes topics to appropriate human/business sources.

## Phase 5 — Business AI assistant (months 24+)

**Goal:** help businesses create and improve, without making hidden decisions for them.

- draft descriptions, offers and campaign content for business approval;
- explain analytics, customer interest and possible improvements;
- recommend marketing actions based on measured, permissioned insights.

Business AI should not expose individual private user behavior or make unsupported performance promises.

## Phase 6 — Constrained AI agents (future)

Agents may compare options, prepare a booking request or help a business prepare a response only within explicit permissions, confirmed actions and audit trails. They must have scoped tools, clear stop conditions, user/business approval before external commitments and reliable human escalation. Autonomous financial, legal or consequential decisions are out of scope without dedicated review.

## Infrastructure evolution

| Capability | Supporting layer |
| --- | --- |
| Local knowledge | verified businesses, services, locations and relationships |
| Semantic understanding | approved embeddings and vector retrieval |
| Ranking/prediction | evaluated recommendation and ML components |
| Improvement loop | user action → result → feedback → measured improvement |

## Quality and safety metrics

- **Accuracy:** are retrieved facts and matches correct?
- **Helpfulness:** does the user reach a useful next action?
- **Trust:** do explanations, controls and outcomes maintain confidence?
- **Connection impact:** does AI improve successful user–business connections?
- **Safety:** privacy incidents, unsupported claims, failure rate and escalation outcomes.

## Principles

- **Human first:** AI serves people and businesses, not engagement alone.
- **Transparency:** say when AI is used and explain recommendations appropriately.
- **Privacy:** use data only for permitted purposes and controls.
- **Continuous evaluation:** improve from evidence, monitoring and feedback.

## Final principle

The most valuable AI is not the one that speaks most elegantly. It is the one that accurately understands local life, respects trust and helps people make better decisions.
