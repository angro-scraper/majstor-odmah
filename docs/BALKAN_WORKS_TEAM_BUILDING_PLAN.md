# Balkan.works Team Building Plan

## Purpose

Define how Balkan.works builds a team from founding stage to regional organization. The goal is to hire the right people at the right moment — not titles, but accountable owners of meaningful outcomes.

```text
Founders → Core team → Functional teams → Regional organization → Global standard
```

## Phase 1 — Founding team (months 0–12)

**Goal:** build the core product and prove the market.

| Role | Accountable for |
| --- | --- |
| CEO / Founder | vision, strategy, capital, partners and culture |
| CTO / Technical Founder | architecture, delivery, infrastructure and technical standards |
| Product Lead | user problem, roadmap, prioritization and learning |
| Growth Lead | users, marketing experiments and channel evidence |

In the earliest stage, responsibilities may be combined. Hire only when a recurring bottleneck prevents the founders from learning, shipping, acquiring supply or serving customers.

## Phase 2 — Core team (months 12–24)

**Goal:** turn a product into a repeatable operating company.

- **Engineering:** backend, frontend, mobile when validated, QA.
- **Product:** product manager, UX design and user research.
- **Growth:** marketing, SEO and content.
- **Sales:** business acquisition and account management.
- **Operations:** customer support and operating management.

Sequence roles according to the largest proven constraint. Do not fill a complete org chart before the work, demand or revenue requires it.

## Phase 3 — Scale team (years 2–5)

**Goal:** support regional growth without lowering product or operating quality.

Leadership roles can include VP Engineering, VP Growth, VP Sales and VP Operations. Each leader owns a measurable outcome, develops successors and keeps decisions connected to the company’s strategy, not only their function.

## Phase 4 — Regional organization

```text
HQ → Country teams → City teams → Community network
```

- **Country Manager:** local P&L/result, market execution and partnerships.
- **Local Sales:** business network quality, activation and monetization.
- **Local Growth:** users, relevant content and community activation.

Country and city teams are launched only after a repeatable city playbook, support model, localization requirements and clear operating metrics exist.

## Hiring principles

1. **Hire owners:** people who take responsibility for a result.
2. **Hire learners:** learning speed matters more than static knowledge in a changing company.
3. **Hire builders:** favor people who turn ambiguity into useful work.
4. **Hire for culture:** skills can grow; integrity, customer focus and ownership are harder to change.

## Interview and performance system

Assess problem solving, ownership, execution and clear communication through job-relevant work, structured interviews and consistent evaluation criteria. Each team member needs clear goals, measurable outcomes, expectations and regular feedback on results, growth and next steps. Follow applicable employment and anti-discrimination laws in every market.

## Culture and communication

- **Customer first:** begin with real user and business outcomes.
- **Move fast:** learn and correct quickly without compromising safety or quality.
- **Think long term:** build systems and trust that compound.
- **Own the outcome:** each person makes progress visible and resolves blockers.

Default to documenting important decisions, processes, learnings and standards. Meetings need an agenda, owner and decision or next action. The knowledge system belongs to the company, not to individual inboxes.

## Leadership principle

The strongest leaders do not create dependency. They create clarity, raise standards and develop more leaders.

## Final vision

Balkan.works does not need a large team for its own sake. It needs an exceptional, accountable team capable of building a system that supports substantial long-term growth.
