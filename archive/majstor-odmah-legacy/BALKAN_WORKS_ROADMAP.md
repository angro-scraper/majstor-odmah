# Balkan.works Roadmap

## Product strategy

Balkan.works develops one modular regional super-app Core that connects users, businesses, offers, services and business tools. It does not create twenty disconnected products.

## Phase 0 — Foundation (1–2 months)

Core platform: authentication, users, profiles, roles, permissions, countries, cities and locations.

Infrastructure: repository, CI/CD, Docker, database, environment management, logging and monitoring.

Design system: UI component library, colors, typography, cards and navigation.

**Outcome:** an empty, secure platform ready for modules.

## Phase 1 — First Product (3–6 months)

Deals is the first daily-use reason to install: digital flyers, business-created offers, location/category search and favorites. Business profiles provide location, working hours and published offers. Global search covers businesses, offers and categories.

**Outcome:** first users and first businesses.

## Phase 2 — Consumer Growth (6–12 months)

Save Food adds surplus packages, reservations and pickup codes. Rewards adds points, levels and referrals. Notifications add local actions, new offers and permission-aware recommendations.

**Outcome:** Balkan.works becomes a daily-use product.

## Phase 3 — Business Platform (12–18 months)

Opsnestone integration provides inventory, products, sales, invoices and analytics. Business subscriptions add Free, Basic, Pro and Enterprise plans. Marketing Tools enable campaigns, offers and results tracking.

**Outcome:** Balkan.works becomes a business operating tool.

## Phase 4 — Marketplace Expansion

Services: craftspeople, cleaning, repair and professionals. Jobs: local, freelance and seasonal work. Marketplace: products and local commerce.

**Outcome:** people and the local economy connect through one platform.

## Phase 5 — Money Layer

Wallet, cashback and rewards; regulated in-app payments, subscriptions and commissions; Stock Radar financial education, market information and portfolio tracking. These are enabled only after provider, legal and security approval.

## Phase 6 — AI Layer

Balkan AI Assistant performs permission-aware discovery and recommendations: nearby affordable markets, local offers and available services. It may draft an inquiry or reservation, but critical actions remain confirmed by the user.

## Phase 7 — Regional Expansion

Priority expansion: Serbia, Croatia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Montenegro, Slovenia and diaspora, followed by additional local languages and partners.

## Phase 8 — Advanced Modules

Health, Auto, Real Estate, Travel and Education are enabled only after prior phases demonstrate user need, partner readiness and feature-flag review.

## Success metrics

| Horizon | Users | Businesses |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| First year | 100,000 | 5,000 |
| Three years | 1,000,000 | 50,000 |
| Five years | Regional super-app | Regional super-app |

## Development rules

1. Do not build a module without user need.
2. Every module uses Core Platform and Balkan ID.
3. Every module is independently feature-flagged.
4. Daily-use value has priority.
5. Business and consumer must both benefit.

## Final vision

Digital infrastructure for the Balkans: one account, one application and one ecosystem where people buy, save, work, operate businesses and communicate.
