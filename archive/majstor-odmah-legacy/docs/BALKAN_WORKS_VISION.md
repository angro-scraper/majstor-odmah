# Balkan.works — Product Vision

## Mission

Balkan.works builds a digital ecosystem for the Balkans that connects citizens, businesses and local communities through one platform. It is infrastructure for daily life and regional business, not a collection of unrelated applications.

## The platform promise

**One account. One identity. One ecosystem.**

The platform serves consumers, businesses and the diaspora through **Balkan ID**. It owns the Core, navigation, permissions, local discovery, trust, rewards and future integration contracts. External products connect underneath it and never replace its identity layer.

## Principles

### Modularity

- Every capability is an independent module with its own API boundary.
- Users activate only the modules they need and arrange their own dashboard.
- New modules are added without changing the Core identity, permission or location layers.

### Local relevance

The product must support country, city, municipality, business and user context. Primary markets are Serbia, Croatia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Montenegro, North Macedonia and Slovenia. Albania, Bulgaria and Romania follow, together with diaspora communities in Germany, Austria and Switzerland.

### Business first

Businesses create the supply side of the platform: offers, services, products, jobs and local activity. The Business Cloud is therefore equal in importance to the Consumer Cloud.

### Sustainability

The platform helps reduce food waste, digitalize small businesses and strengthen local economies. Sustainability claims are always measurable and transparently labelled.

## Product clouds

### Consumer Cloud

| Module | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Deals | Digital flyers, actions, coupons and local offers. |
| Save Food | Surplus-food packages, reservations and lower-cost shopping. |
| Services | Craftspeople, home services, repair and local professionals. |
| Jobs | Local, freelance and seasonal work. |
| Marketplace | Local listings, products, second-hand goods and small businesses. |
| Rewards | Shared points, coupons, rewards and future cashback. |

### Business Cloud

Opsnestone is the business operating layer for small and medium businesses: bookkeeping, invoices, inventory, CRM, analytics and sales. Marketing Tools provide flyers, campaigns, push notifications and loyalty. Analytics provides business, customer, sales, offer and campaign views.

### Money Cloud

Wallet, transactions, subscriptions, cashback and later payment rails live here. Stock Radar is the future information and financial education connector. Regulated money movement remains disconnected until approved providers and contracts are configured.

### AI Cloud

Balkan Assistant will use natural language to help users discover offers, products, services, businesses and jobs. It is activated only after a secure model-provider configuration and must operate through Balkan.works permissions.

## Balkan ID

Each user owns one Balkan ID containing profile, activity history, points, reputation and verifications. No module or future connector creates a competing login.

## Long-term goal

Become the leading digital platform for everyday life and business in the Balkans: an application people open daily and an operating tool that helps local businesses grow efficiently.

## Delivery rule

Every future task is evaluated against this document. The complete native modular system is built first; API keys, external platforms and payment connections are enabled later through their dedicated integration layers.
