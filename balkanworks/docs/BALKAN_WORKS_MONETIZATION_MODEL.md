# Balkan.works Monetization Model

## Principle

Do not charge for basic presence or bury consumers in advertising. Charge businesses and partners for clearly measured visibility, workflow tools, automation, outcomes and enterprise value.

## Revenue layers

| Layer | Customer | Value exchanged | Rollout gate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Business visibility | Local business | Featured placement, sponsored offers and campaign reach | Supply quality and transparent ranking labels |
| Business SaaS | Small/medium business | Offers, campaigns, CRM, analytics and operations | Business Portal proves repeat use |
| Opsnestone | Business/enterprise | Inventory, invoices, customer operations and reporting | Product/security readiness and support model |
| Save Food | Food partner | Successful reservation facilitation | Payment, cancellation and consumer terms validated |
| Marketplace | Seller | Transaction completion, promotion and analytics | Marketplace trust and payment flow ready |
| AI services | Users/businesses/enterprise | Advanced assistance and approved automation | Measurable quality, consent and usage controls |
| Partnerships | Strategic partner | Integration, co-marketing and aggregated insights | Contract, security and privacy review |

## Plans

- **Free:** business profile, location and limited core offer capability.
- **Starter:** additional offers and basic analytics.
- **Growth:** campaigns, advanced reporting and customer/business tools.
- **Pro / Enterprise:** Opsnestone, governed AI, integrations, service levels and negotiated controls.

Plans must stay understandable, country-aware and easy to cancel. Sponsored results are visibly labeled and cannot masquerade as organic relevance.

## Revenue roadmap

1. Digital Flyers and transparent sponsored placement.
2. Business subscriptions and Opsnestone SaaS after validated workflow value.
3. Save Food/Marketplace transaction fees only with compliant payment, fulfilment and support flows.
4. Premium AI and enterprise integrations after demonstrated model quality and human control.

## Data and partner boundaries

Personal data is never sold. Business insight products use aggregated, privacy-reviewed trends. Enterprise integration pricing never grants a customer access to other tenants' data or platform-wide personal behavior.

## Unit economics and guardrails

Track customer acquisition cost, business/customer lifetime value, ARPU, contribution margin, churn, conversion and retained usage. Avoid dark patterns, forced subscriptions, hidden fees, excessive notifications and paywalls around core safety or account controls.

## Decision rule

Every paid feature must answer: What measurable value does it create? Who pays? How is the price transparent? What is the support/refund path? Does it improve, rather than weaken, consumer trust and the local business network?
