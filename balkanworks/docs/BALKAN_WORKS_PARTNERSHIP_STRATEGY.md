# Balkan.works Partnership Strategy

## Purpose

Build a regional partner network in which every integration creates clear value for both the partner and Balkan.works: distribution, trust and local relevance for the platform; customers, digital tools and analytics for the partner.

## Partner categories

| Category | Primary value | Initial offer | Integration boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Local businesses, restaurants and shops | Daily local supply | Business profile, offers, flyers, Save Food | Business Portal and consented analytics |
| Retail and food chains | Promotions and waste reduction | Catalogs, campaigns, loyalty and Save Food | Partner API after pilot validation |
| Business associations | Business acquisition | Member onboarding and training | Managed partner dashboard |
| Cities and community organizations | Local economy and events | Local offers, city campaigns and tourism | City-specific content workspace |
| Education institutions | Student adoption and skills | Student programs and business education | Community campaign tools |
| Banks and telecoms | Scale and trusted distribution | Co-marketing, business digitalization, rewards | No payment or identity integration before legal/security review |
| Strategic regional partners | Ecosystem expansion | Custom campaigns and negotiated integrations | Versioned Partner API and SLA |

## Tiers

1. **Community Partner** — visibility and jointly approved campaigns.
2. **Business Partner** — promotions, catalog onboarding and standard integration options.
3. **Strategic Partner** — large-scale distribution, custom integrations and governed account management.

Tier is not a shortcut around platform trust, privacy or moderation requirements.

## Acquisition process

`Research → Qualified contact → Discovery → Pilot → Measure → Scale`

Every pilot has a named owner, written objective, target geography, duration, consent basis, success metric and exit plan. Production integrations follow security, data-processing and legal review.

## Partner dashboard and API

The future Partner Dashboard will expose only the partner's own campaign and aggregated performance data:

- campaigns, offers, catalog status and moderation state;
- attribution, impressions, activations, reservations and retention;
- approved API credentials, rate limits and webhook history;
- billing/subscription information when payments are introduced.

Partner APIs are versioned, scoped and feature-flagged. No partner receives raw personal data, cross-partner data or unapproved messaging capability.

## Value propositions

- **Small business:** reach new customers without complicated marketing.
- **Large business:** participate in the Balkan digital network with reliable reporting.
- **City:** strengthen local commerce and community discovery.
- **Food partner:** turn surplus into measurable savings and lower waste.

## Metrics

Measure sourced users, activated businesses, campaign engagement, reservations/sales where consented, recurring use, partner retention and verified revenue impact. Evaluate pilots by outcomes, not vanity reach.

## Rollout priority

1. Local businesses, restaurants, bakeries and stores.
2. Business associations, education and city/community partners.
3. Banks, telecoms and national/regional chains after Core, privacy and security controls are proven.

## Operating principle

The network is the platform's durable advantage. Technology enables it, but each partnership must remain voluntary, transparent, locally useful and measurable.
