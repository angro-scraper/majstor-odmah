# Balkan.works Brand Guidelines

## Foundation

**Balkan.works** connects the region, people, culture and local opportunity with work, creation, business and practical usefulness.

**Vision:** become trusted digital infrastructure that connects the Balkans.

**Mission:** help people find value around them and help businesses grow through accessible technology.

**Positioning:** the digital bridge between people and local businesses.

## Brand promise

- **For people:** “Pronađi ono što ti treba blizu sebe.”
- **For businesses:** “Budite tamo gde vas ljudi traže.”
- **For partners:** “Povežimo lokalne zajednice kroz tehnologiju.”

## Values

1. **Connection** — connect people with opportunities.
2. **Trust** — earn confidence through accurate, useful information.
3. **Local power** — local businesses and communities matter.
4. **Innovation** — use technology to make everyday life simpler.
5. **Growth** — help people and businesses progress.

## Voice and language

Speak clearly, directly and humanly. Be friendly, reliable, modern, simple and useful. Avoid complex business jargon, cold corporate language and claims that cannot be proven.

Good: “Pronađi proverene firme u svom gradu.” / “Rastite tamo gde vas ljudi već traže.”

Avoid: “revolucionarno”, “savršeno”, “garantovano”, “najjeftinije” and exaggerated AI/market claims.

Preferred language: povezivanje, lokalno, zajedno, rast, poverenje, prilika.

## Audience messages

| Audience | Core message |
| --- | --- |
| Users | Brže pronađi ono što ti treba. |
| Businesses | Privuci nove mušterije i razvijaj posao. |
| Partners | Povežimo lokalne zajednice kroz tehnologiju. |

## Visual direction

The experience is modern, clean, digital and approachable. It should create safety, simplicity and trust. Use the Design Language for exact tokens, components, typography and accessibility; these guidelines define the brand intent behind them.

Logo concepts should express connection, network, regional identity and progress. Icons remain simple, clear and functional. Use real people, local businesses and authentic situations when imagery is needed; avoid generic stock scenes.

## Content and social

Create useful, local, educational and positive content around:

- Local stories from businesses and people.
- Business growth advice.
- Community activity and local opportunities.
- Clear explanations of how technology/AI helps, without hype.

## Consistency and trust

Website, apps, support, marketing and partnerships use the same promise, language and honesty. Trust comes from accurate information, transparent conditions, quality content and a reliable user experience — never just from visual branding.

## Extensions

Future products may use the same master brand: Balkan.works AI, Balkan.works Business, Balkan.works Wallet and Balkan.works Marketplace. They inherit Core voice and visual foundations; they do not become unrelated brands.
