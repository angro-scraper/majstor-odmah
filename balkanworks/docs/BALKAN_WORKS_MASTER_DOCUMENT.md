# Balkan.works Master Document

> **Project source of truth for vision and principles.**
>
> Document hierarchy: this Master Document defines direction and non-negotiable principles; the Master Roadmap defines delivery order; the MVP PRD defines what must work for the MVP release; technical strategy documents define implementation constraints.

## Project overview

**Vision:** build a regional digital ecosystem connecting people, local businesses, business tools and artificial intelligence.

**Mission:** help people find useful local value and help businesses grow through accessible digitalization.

## Core idea

Balkan.works is a network — people, businesses, governed data and AI — that strengthens the local digital economy. It is not a collection of disconnected applications.

## Problem and solution

Consumers face fragmented information, difficult local discovery, low-trust listings and generic offers. Businesses face weak digital presence, expensive marketing, limited reach and disconnected tools. Balkan.works provides local discovery, trusted business profiles, offers, recommendations, marketing/analytics and future business operations through a shared Core.

## Product ecosystem

`Balkan.works Core → Offers → Business Tools / Digital Flyers → Opsnestone → Marketplace / Wallet → AI Intelligence`

The MVP delivers identity, location, search, businesses, offers, saved items, basic business analytics and moderation. Each later module uses Core contracts and feature flags rather than rebuilding identity, data or trust.

## Users

- **Customer:** local information, offers, services and useful discovery.
- **Business:** customers, visibility, promotion, analytics and operational tools.
- **Admin:** quality, safety, permissioned moderation and platform operations.

## Business model

Business subscriptions, premium visibility, digital marketing/campaigns, qualified transaction fees, premium AI and strategic partnerships. Value precedes monetization; personal data is never sold; prices and sponsored placement are transparent.

## Technology, data and AI

The platform supports mobile and web clients, a modular NestJS Core, PostgreSQL/Prisma, controlled analytics and a permissioned AI Gateway. Data exists to improve experience and business decisions under minimization, consent, access control and audit. AI assists users/businesses with grounded recommendations and drafts; it cannot bypass human approval for impactful actions.

## Security and trust

Protect data, enforce access control, use encryption/TLS, audit privileged activity, isolate business tenants and operate tested backups/recovery. Trust is a product outcome and a release condition.

## Development principles

1. Build simple and modular.
2. Measure important outcomes.
3. Put users and businesses first.
4. Scale only from proven demand.
5. Do not build a feature because it is possible; build it because it increases network value.

## Strategic phases

- **MVP (0–6 months):** first users/businesses in a validated local loop.
- **Growth (6–18 months):** repeatable city network and first sustainable revenue.
- **Platform (2–5 years):** regional business ecosystem and selected modules.
- **AI economy (5+ years):** trusted intelligence and regional infrastructure.

## Team and metrics

Begin with accountable founder, technical, product and business ownership; grow only to solve validated constraints. Track active users, activation/retention, active businesses, offer freshness, paid adoption, business outcomes, network health, data quality, security/reliability and AI usefulness.

## Long-term moat

The durable advantage is a combination of local network density, trust, responsibly governed data, business outcomes and useful AI — not software alone.

## Development rule

Every future change must preserve modular architecture, security/privacy, measurable outcomes and extensibility for future modules. When documents conflict, resolve against the hierarchy at the top of this file and record the decision.
