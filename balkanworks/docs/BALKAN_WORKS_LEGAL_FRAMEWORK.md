# Balkan.works Legal Framework

> Product and operating framework only — not legal advice, a jurisdiction-specific legal opinion or a substitute for qualified counsel. Legal documents and launch decisions require review in every active market.

## Principle

Legal certainty, consumer protection and data rights are product requirements from the beginning. The platform needs clear company, user, business, privacy and operating rules before public launch.

## Required document set

- Terms of Service / user agreement.
- Privacy Policy and rights-request process.
- Cookie Policy and consent management for web analytics/marketing.
- Business Agreement, including data accuracy, offer responsibility and permitted conduct.
- Data Processing Agreement where the legal relationship requires one.
- Content, review/moderation and report-handling policies.
- Partner, employee and contractor agreements covering confidentiality, IP ownership and security obligations.

## Product controls needed

Users need access to, correction of and appropriate deletion/control over their data, subject to lawful retention obligations. Businesses own responsibility for their products, services, offers and customer-facing claims. The platform must state its moderation, suspension, content removal and appeal rules transparently.

Data processing follows lawful purpose, transparency, minimization, security, retention and access controls. Cookie categories distinguish essential, analytics and marketing purposes; non-essential use requires the appropriate consent flow.

## Content and reviews

Prohibit fraudulent information, scams, unlawful offers, harmful/abusive content and manipulation. Reviews need clear rules on authenticity, relation to an actual experience, moderation and dispute/appeal handling. Audit trails support fair, reviewable decisions.

## Future regulated modules

Marketplace requires buyer/seller, refund, complaint, fulfilment and liability terms. Payments/Wallet require qualified providers, transaction records, consumer protection, financial/regulatory review and reconciliation. AI requires disclosure that a user is interacting with AI, purpose/data controls, limitation language and human escalation. No AI makes deceptive guarantees or uses private data beyond approved consent/purpose.

## Liability and incidents

Terms must describe platform scope and the responsibilities of participating businesses without attempting to override mandatory consumer/data laws. Security incidents follow detection → assessment → containment → lawful notification → resolution → improvement, with legal/compliance involvement.

## Ownership and brand

Protect the Balkan.works name, logo, design, code and original content through appropriate ownership, licensing, contractor assignment and brand processes. Validate trademarks, domains and regional naming rights with qualified counsel before relying on exclusivity.

## Launch gate

Before a public country/city launch, complete local legal review of company structure, consumer rules, privacy/cookies, business terms, advertising/promotion law, employment/contractor setup, payment/marketplace implications and incident contacts. Update documents as modules or jurisdictions change.
